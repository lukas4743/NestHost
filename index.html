FAQ

Got questions?

Here are the most common ones. Still unsure? Ask in our Discord.

How do I order a server?

Join our Discord, go to #order-here and fill in the order form. We'll DM you to confirm and get you set up within 24 hours.

How do I pay?

We accept PayPal and bank transfer. Payment details are shared after you submit your order form in Discord.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! Just DM us on Discord anytime and we'll upgrade your server. You only pay the difference for the rest of the month.

What Minecraft versions do you support?

We support all major Java Edition versions. Just let us know which version you need in your order form.

Will my server be online 24/7?

Yes, your server runs continuously. We only take it offline for maintenance which we always announce in advance.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no contracts or commitments. Just DM us before your next billing date and we'll cancel it, no questions asked.
